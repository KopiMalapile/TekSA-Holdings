ABOUT US

TekSA Holdings (Pty) Ltd (“TekSA”) is an IT solutions company that provides businesses with end-to-end services in business analysis, systems development, and project management. We specialize in low-code/no-code solutions and follow an agile methodology for development. Our team works closely with clients to understand their needs and requirements, conducting thorough business analysis to ensure that their technology solutions align with clients' business goals.



We are your trusted partner in navigating the digital landscape. With our newly expanded range of services, TekSA is here to empower your business with innovative technology solutions, strategic investments, and comprehensive risk management services. Our team of experts, backed by cutting-edge technologies and strong industry partnerships, is dedicated to delivering tailored solutions that drive growth, efficiency, and sustainability.
